Chicken Roulade

Savory and plump

This recipe will teach you how to cook the perfect chicken roulade!

Ingredients

  • boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • bacon
  • spinach
  • onions
  • mayonnaise
  • breadcrumbs
  • salt and pepepr

Steps

  1. Dice bacon and onions and sautee
  2. Add spinach and season
  3. Allow to cool
  4. Add mayonnaise and breadcrumbs to stuffing mix and chill
  5. Butterfly chicken thighs
  6. Stuff, roll and bake
