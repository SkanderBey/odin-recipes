Chocolate Chip Cookie

Decadent and chewy

This recipe will teach you how to bake the perfect chocolate chip cookie. All it takes is a little patience and a whole lot of love!

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • granulated sugar
  • vanilla extract
  • flour
  • chocolate chips
  • baking powder

Steps

  1. Cream eggs, sugar and vanilla extract
  2. Mix together flour and baking powder
  3. Add dry ingredients to egg mixture
  4. Mix well
  5. Add chocolate chips
  6. Allow dough to rest
  7. Portion and bake
