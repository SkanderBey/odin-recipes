Cesar Salad

Light and Refreshing

What better way to cool off with a refreshing classic Cesar salad?

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • cherry tomatoes
  • croutons
  • grilled chicken
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Cesar dressing

Steps

  1. Cut Romaine lettuce and grilled chicken into bite size pieces
  2. Toss lettuce, chicken and cherry tomatoes together in a bowl
  3. Add croutons and parmesan cheese
  4. Top with Cesar dressing
